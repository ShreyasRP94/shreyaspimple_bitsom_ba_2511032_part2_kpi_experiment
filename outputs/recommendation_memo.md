## Executive Summary

The objective of this analysis is to determine whether the new onboarding and activation campaign should be launched to all users. The campaign aims to improve user conversion, onboarding completion, engagement, and revenue generation. The recommendation will be based on experimental evidence comparing treatment and control groups, while ensuring that guardrail metrics such as support tickets and refund requests do not deteriorate. Statistical testing and business impact analysis will be used to support the final decision.


## North Star Metric: Paid Conversion Rate

Paid Conversion Rate was selected as the North Star Metric because it directly measures the primary objective of the onboarding campaign: converting users into paying subscribers. Supporting metrics such as onboarding completion, trial starts, engagement score, and revenue help explain user behavior, while guardrail metrics like refund requests and support tickets ensure that conversion improvements are sustainable and do not negatively impact user experience.
